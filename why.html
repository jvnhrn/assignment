WHY

For my Bachelor project I built a prototype for an app and website. During this project
it became clear to me that I want to work in the field of UX and UI. To better position myself in this area,
I want to learn how to not only design websites and apps, but also how to build them .
I want to understand all steps up to a finished product. I don't only want to design portotypes,
but also websites and apps that already work. I chose the Talent Garden Code Master Bootcamp for my further education,
because on the one hand I like the way the course is structured and on the other hand experts from
the field of Front End Development will teach the content. I was also very impressed by Talent Garden's claim that
the career of the students is in the foreground. I would like to specialise in UX and programming with this course.
I want to design user-oriented, economically responsible projects.
